WHO WE ARE

.

Small business directory and eComm

A small business directory and eCommerce platform that houses vibrant startup enterprises. We view ourselves as an open and diversified online marketplace, as seen by the wide range of products, services, ideas and skills being traded by hosted webstores

Enable seamless online shopping for rural communities

Born during the times of Covid-19, Yekee's existence is to enable a seamless shopping experience on the one hand, while addressing the plight of would be young entrepreneurs who's hopes of either starting or growing their small businesses had been dashed by the South african economic slump as a result of this desease, on the other hand

Marketplace of choice

The platform provides access to the buying market. We aim to be the online marketplace of choice for both the shopper and the seller. Also, due to socio-economic disparities in our otherwise wonderful country, the platform has a self imposed obligation to host 50% of woman webshops.
